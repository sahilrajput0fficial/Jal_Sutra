Heavy Metal Concentrations

Batch Upload (CSV/XLSX)

Upload a CSV or Excel file with columns: sampleId, date, depth, location, latitude, longitude, lead, cadmium, chromium, arsenic, mercury. Values for metals should be in mg/L (the uploader can accept µg/L or ppm if you include columns like leadUnit, cadmiumUnit, etc.)
